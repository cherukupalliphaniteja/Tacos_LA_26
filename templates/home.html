Since 2001

Our Story

Tacos LA 26 started back in 2001 with one stand, one grill, and a goal — make good tacos, no shortcuts. What began as a small family setup on Avenue 26 turned into something bigger when the neighborhood showed up hungry.

Soon, the whole block filled with food, music, and people — and our little stand became part of LA's street food story. Over two decades later, we're still here, still cooking over open flame, still using the same recipes.
